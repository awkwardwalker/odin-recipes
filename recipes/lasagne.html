Lasagne

Description

Italian dish made from minced beef, tomatoes and pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • 500g of minced beef
  • 1 White Onion
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 400g Chopped Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in a pan
  2. Cook the onions and the garlic
  3. Add chopped tomatoes
  4. Put Lasagne sheets on top and chuck in the oven
