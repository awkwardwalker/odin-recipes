Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

"Indian Dish". We say this because, well, it was more likely to have been invented in the UK! Fragrant Chicken that is spiced in a tandorri spice mix then cooked in a tandoor oven.

Then cooked in a masala sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • 1 White Onion
  • 1 Tikka spice mix
  • Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 400g Chopped Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken with the spice mix
  2. Cook the onions and the garlic
  3. Add chopped tomatoes
  4. Chuck chicken in and simmer for 20 minutes
